Experiment 5: 3D Graphics

Imitate

I was inspired by the professor’s example during class, where he had a tornado as an image background, and teapots going in a circular rotation. I don’t have the image, but I am going to include a picture of a tornado. So the 3D art that I want to do is have 3D objects of sheep (from Blender) jumping over a fence, which would help the user fall asleep. To include another form of 3D art, I’ll create a sphere that rotates in a circular motion.

Integrate

For now, I added the blender object to the p5.js project. I intended to add a green rectangle at the bottom of the screen, but I am still trying to figure out how to do that. Not only that, I tried adding a sphere to the project, but every time I did it, it would combine with the sheep, which was not the plan. After including the sheep model, I now have a night background. I genuinely like how this came out, so what I am going to do in step three is make it so that I can spawn multiple sheep. Hopefully, after that, they can jump over the forest, to mimic the sheep jumping over the fence.
