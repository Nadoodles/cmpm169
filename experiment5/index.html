Reflection

I worked on this assignment independently. I was inspired by the fact that when I was starting this assignment, I was really tired, so the first thing that came to my mind was counting sheep with a goodnight song. The blender object of the sheep was bought, and the moon was originally a sphere with a moon PNG. While I utilized ChatGPT to address some bugs, the majority of the work is my own. I dedicated numerous hours to coding, seeking feedback and opinions from friends along the way. The most challenging part of the project was attempting to create the background scenery. Every time I tried to create a rectangle box, for some reason, it wouldn’t allow me. After multiple tries, I gave up on the rectangle box and looked up an image of a grass field during nighttime. Another issue I had was positioning issues, but it was resolved when I tried many values. In the end, I am pleased with the result, and I believe I have done a commendable job on this assignment.
