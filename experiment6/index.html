Reflection

I worked on this assignment independently. When this assignment was presented, I remembered watching YouTube videos of artists creating landscapes, portraits, and images using words as a drawing mechanism instead of coloring, painting, or drawing. It caught my attention, so in this assignment, I attempted to recreate it with three art models of anime characters. While I utilized ChatGPT to address some bugs, the majority of the work is my own. I dedicated numerous hours to coding, seeking feedback, and opinions from friends along the way. The most challenging part was incorporating Tracery. However, it didn’t work out, and I completely removed it from the plan. It’s not an issue, but since the images are large and take a while to load, when you press either 0, 1, or 2, please take into consideration that it may take some time for the image to load. There’s no issue with the code; it just takes a long time to create the art.
